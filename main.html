Let's explore the world of Folktales!

Folktales are traditional stories that are passed down orally from
generation to generation. They are created by "the folk", or
the people, and often feature magical or supernatural elements.
These stories can explain something or provide moral lessons.
They are ussually fictional and may involve talking animals,
royalty, peasants, or mythical creatures.

Why should you read it?

  • If you want to learn something new about the folklore
  • If you want to find more interesting stories from different cultures
  • If you want to learn some lessons out of it
  • If you want to enjoy an interesting story
